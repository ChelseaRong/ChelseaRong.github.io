Hey, I'm
Li (Chelsea) Rong A Dancer-Choreographer A Software Engineer

Graduated from UK's oldest dance conservatory Rambert School of Ballet and Contemporary Dance. Chelsea has choreographed and performed at a range of venues including the Chelsea Theatre in London, YunFeng Theatre in Shanghai, le Studio 24 in Caen and more. Aside from dance, Chelsea worked as a professional software engineer and currently studies beaux-arts at l'école supérieure d'arts & médias de Caen.

Duality

Duality was a dance performance inspired by the similarities and differences between contemporary and capoeira movements. Coming from two different historical context, the staggering parallels in their movement patterns brought the foundation in developping the piece. Duality was performed initially during the Chelsea and Kensington Festival 2021, and again during Hush Festival in 2023.

Aqua

Aqua is a video installation inspired by how water droplets behaves under harmonic frequencies. Using generative coding and based on a Chinese ink drawing aesthetic, the final visual features original choreography. A live version of the choreography was performed at the Anya Linden Studio Theatre.

Bird’s eye, a tree and the stream

Bird’s eye, a tree and the stream, photography using charcoal and water with organic processes like drying and lighting. Charcoal contains carbon, an element that is essential to all life forms on earth, but is also the source of many modern malaise. Using charcoal to represent the natural landscape and its constituents, this work intend to discuss the dialectical nature of carbon.

Magia Naturalis

Using generative coding, and photographs from everyday life, this video installation intends to showcase nature's wonders in a psychedelic style. Note that the sample you are seeing here is optimized for rendering, not quality.
